About the Movie

The Dark Knight is a 2008 superhero film directed, produced, and co-written by Christopher Nolan. The sequel to 2005's Batman Begins, it is the second installment of Nolan's The Dark Knight Trilogy.

With Batman taking on the Joker, a madman with a nihilistic agenda, Gotham City descends into chaos, and it is up to Batman to stop him from bringing it to ruin.

Cast

  • Christian Bale as Bruce Wayne / Batman
  • Heath Ledger as Joker
  • Aaron Eckhart as Harvey Dent / Two-Face
  • Michael Caine as Alfred Pennyworth
  • Gary Oldman as James Gordon
  • Maggie Gyllenhaal as Rachel Dawes
